About Fulshear TSA

Here in Fulshear TSA we empower students to explore STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) through hands-on competitions, leadership opportunities, and real-world problem solving. By joining Fulshear TSA, students develop critical thinking, collaboration, and technical skills that prepare them for future careers. It also offers a chance to connect with like-minded peers and showcase innovations at regional, state, and national conferences. TSA is a fun and inspiring way to turn your passion for technology into real achievement.
