Fulshear High School Technology Student Association

Fulshear TSA gives students the chance to learn about science, technology, engineering, and math through competitions, leadership, and hands on activities. Members build teamwork, problem solving, and leadership skills while preparing for future careers. TSA also helps students make new friends, compete at conferences, and have fun while learning.

Awards & Recognitions

Fulshear TSA has built a strong tradition of achievement, leadership, and service. Our chapter is proud to recognize student accomplishments in competition, leadership, and academic growth.

  • Multiple state champions and top-performing teams in Texas and National TSA competitions.
  • This year, one of our teams earned a national TSA event win, highlighting the strength of our chapter.
  • We have also earned the Chapter Excellence Award being one of the chapter who would have the largest chapter memberships in the state competition!
  • Students can also pursue National TSA scholarship opportunities, student achievement awards, and recognition programs highlighted on the National TSA website.
  • Our chapter encourages students to explore State Officer opportunities and the Texas TSA Achievement Program, including Stateman and Lone Star Degree testing.

Interested in learning more about achievement opportunities or degree testing? Reach out to our officers for details.

What We Do

We design, build, and compete in state-level events, develop technical projects, and practice problem solving with teammates. Our chapter focuses on real-world skills, event preparation, and community leadership.

Our Goal

Our goal is to prepare students for future careers in STEM while building confidence, creativity, and collaboration. We support every member as they learn, grow, and represent Fulshear High School with pride.

Student Board

Our student board is led by elected officers who organize meetings, coordinate competitions, and guide chapter activities. These leaders represent the club, support members, and help shape our TSA experience.
